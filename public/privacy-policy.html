1. Introduction

1.1 Purpose

This Privacy Policy describes how [Ninja Deliveries Private Limited] ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use our website, mobile application, or any related services (collectively, the "Services").

1.2 Acceptance

By accessing or using our Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue the use of our Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Registration: When you create an account, we collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and password.
  • Order Information: When placing an order, we may collect your delivery address, payment information (e.g., credit card details, billing address), and any other details necessary to fulfill your order.
  • Communications: If you contact our customer support or communicate with us via email, chat, or other channels, we may collect your contact information and any other personal information you choose to provide.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device and browser you use to access our Services, such as your IP address, operating system, browser type, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect data about your usage of our Services, including the date and time you visited, the features you used, and the pages you viewed.
  • Location Information: If you enable location services on your device, we may collect your precise or approximate location to facilitate order delivery, improve our Services, and provide location-based offers.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media: If you choose to log in through or otherwise connect a social media account (e.g., Facebook, Google), we may collect information from that platform in accordance with your privacy settings on that platform.
  • Service Providers: We may receive information about you from our third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, marketing partners) to help us operate, analyze, and improve our Services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use personal information for the following purposes:

3.1 Providing Our Services

To process your orders, arrange deliveries, communicate with you regarding your orders, and provide customer support.

3.2 Account Management

To create and manage your user account, including verifying your identity and maintaining your profile information.

3.3 Payment Processing

To process payments for orders, refunds, or other transactions related to our Services.

3.4 Personalization & Marketing

To personalize content, offers, and recommendations to your interests; send you promotional communications and relevant advertising about our products, services, or special offers. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

3.5 Analytics & Improvements

To analyze how users interact with our Services; troubleshoot issues; and improve the functionality, performance, and security of our Services.

3.6 Legal Compliance & Security

To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes; respond to legal requests; detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or other unlawful activities; and ensure the security of our Services.

4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

4.1 Use of Cookies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with our Services. This information helps us understand usage patterns and improve our Services.

4.2 Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the operation of our website and mobile application (e.g., enabling you to log in to secure areas).
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: Allow us to recognize and count visitors and see how they move around our Services.
  • Functionality Cookies: Used to recognize you when you return to our Services, enabling us to personalize content for you.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Record your visit to our Services, the pages you have visited, and the links you have followed. We may use this information to make our ads more relevant to your interests.

4.3 Cookie Management

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our Services.
